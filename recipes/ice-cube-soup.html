Ice Cube Soup

Description

Ice Cube Soup is one of if not the most economical variations of soup. I am a gargantuan fan of this meal, especially on a hot summer day.

Ingredients

  • Ice Cubes
  • Water
  • Bowl

Steps

  • Take bowl
  • Put water in bowl
  • Grab cubes
  • Put cubes in bowl
  • Devour!
