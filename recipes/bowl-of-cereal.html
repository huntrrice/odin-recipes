Bowl of Cereal

Description

In this guide I will explain how to cook cereal. Cereal is a yummy meal and can be made in many variations. By the end of this tutorial, you should be able to make a majority of cereal varieties.

Ingredients

  • Bowl or other object for holding liquid
  • Spoon (if preferred)
  • Cereal, I will be using fruit loops
  • Milk or any other liquid

Steps

  • Grab liquid holding object
  • Grab cereal
  • Grab liquid
  • Put cereal and liquid in liquid holding object
  • Dinner is served
